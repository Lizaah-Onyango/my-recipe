COOKING MEAT

Ingridients

  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Green chilies
  • Spices (such as cumin, coriander, and turmeric)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooking oil
    •
  1. Prepare the meat: Cut the beef into small pieces and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pan: Heat a small amount of oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Sauté onions and garlic: Add sliced onions and minced garlic to the pan and sauté until the onions are soft.
  4. Add ginger and tomatoes: Add grated ginger and diced tomatoes to the pan and sauté for a few minutes.
  5. Add spices: Add cumin, coriander, and turmeric to the pan and sauté for a few seconds.
  6. Add the meat: Add the seasoned beef pieces to the pan and stir to coat with the spices and vegetables.
  7. Cook the meat: Cook the meat over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until it is browned on all sides.
  8. Add water: Add a small amount of water to the pan and cover with a lid. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 1-2 hours, or until the meat is tender.
  9. Serve: Serve the nyama hot, garnished with green chilies and onions if desired.
